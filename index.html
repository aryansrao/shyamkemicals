Colored Naphthalene Balls

With the same pest-repelling efficiency as our white refined balls, these color-refined naphthalene balls add a vibrant touch to your storage. Their slow-release vapors protect against moths, mold, and mildew while ensuring long-lasting freshness.

White Refined Naphthalene

Formulated with highly purified naphthalene, these white refined balls release a powerful vapor that repels moths, insects, and pests. Perfect for preserving your clothes, books, and storage spaces while eliminating unwanted odors.

Creosote Oil

Derived from coal tar, creosote oil is a powerful wood preservative and anti-fungal agent. Its deep penetration into timber protects against rot, decay, and insect damage, making it essential for industrial and outdoor applications.

Hot Pressed Naphthalene

Manufactured under high-pressure heat, these hot-pressed naphthalene also knowns as HPM are denser and longer-lasting. Their gradual release of vapors provides extended protection against pests, making them ideal for long-term storage solutions.

Naphthalene Powder

Highly refined and versatile, naphthalene powder acts as a robust pest repellent and raw material in chemical production. Ideal for protecting stored goods or as a key ingredient in dyes, resins, and other industrial applications.
